About Me

Hi, I am Runsong Zhu (朱润松), a third-year PhD student in CSE, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Chi-Wing Fu, Philip and Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng. Before that, I received my master's degree in LIESMARS, Wuhan University supervised by Prof. Bisheng Yang and Prof. Zhen Dong in 2022 and bachelor's degree in Central South University in 2019.

My research is focused on 3D Vision, specifically, I currently work on the topic of multi-view image reconstruction.
